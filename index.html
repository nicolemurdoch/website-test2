What's the draw?

This neighborhood is well worth a visit if you can say yes to any of the following:

  • You are a foodie
  • You like leaving manhattan
  • You enjoy waterfront parks, museums, and the borough of Queens!

Check out a great group of local activists who maintain some of those beautiful green spaces, the Astoria Park Alliance!
